### [6] | [AT 2026epw: Discovery of a possible nova from Vera C. Rubin Observatory](https://www.astronomerstelegram.org/?read=17703)
- **日期**: 27 Feb 2026 UT | **源**: `AT 2026epw`

- **概览**: 薇拉·鲁宾天文台发现了一颗疑似新星的暂现源 AT 2026epw。
